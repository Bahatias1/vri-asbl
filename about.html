About Us

Welcome to Virunga Relief Initiative Stablished Since 2024

Virunga Relief Initiative was founded in 2024 with the aim of addressing the critical humanitarian needs of displaced and vulnerable populations in the North Kivu region, specifically in Goma. Faced with considerable challenges, including armed conflicts, forced displacement, and environmental threats, we made a commitment to provide immediate and sustainable assistance to those most in need.

Our Mission is to provide vital support to communities affected by crises in the Virunga region, by offering access to essential needs such as clean water, food, shelter, and healthcare. We also pledge to promote community resilience, strengthen local capacities, and protect the fragile environment of the region.

At Virunga Relief Initiative, we are guided by fundamental values that drive each of our actions:
Commitment to human dignity: We believe in the intrinsic value of every individual and strive to respect, protect, and promote the dignity of all those we serve.
Integrity and transparency: We are committed to acting with integrity and transparency in all our operations, ensuring that resources are used responsibly and effectively.
Collaboration and partnership: We recognize the importance of collaboration and partnership to maximize our impact and achieve our common goals.
Environmental sustainability: We are determined to protect the natural environment of Virunga and promote sustainable practices in all our activities.

Served Over 0 Children in DR CONGO

At Virunga Relief Initiative, we are proud to have positively impacted the lives of over 1,805 children across the Democratic Republic of Congo. Our efforts are focused in key regions including Goma, Bunia, Kalemie, and soon expanding to Lubumbashi.
